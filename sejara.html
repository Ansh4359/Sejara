❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've answered the most common ones below.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ)? ▾
Our standard MOQ is 500 pieces per product. For custom printed items, the MOQ is 5,000 pieces. Wedding/party combos have no MOQ — order for even 10 people!
Do you deliver across all of India? ▾
Yes! We deliver to all 28 states and 8 Union Territories. Delivery is free for orders above ₹50,000. Standard delivery takes 3-7 days depending on your location. Express delivery is available for metro cities.
Can I get samples before placing a bulk order? ▾
Absolutely! We provide free samples for serious buyers. You just pay the shipping cost (typically ₹100-200). Samples are dispatched within 24 hours. This way you can check quality before committing.
What payment methods do you accept? ▾
We accept Bank Transfer (NEFT/RTGS), UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Cheque, and Cash. For regular business clients, we also offer credit terms (30 days) after the first 3 successful orders. GST invoice provided for all transactions.
Are your products eco-friendly and government compliant? ▾
Yes! Our areca leaf, bagasse, and paper products are 100% biodegradable and comply with the government's single-use plastic ban. All food-contact products are FSSAI approved and made from food-grade materials.
Can you print my brand logo on the products? ▾
Yes! We offer custom branding on cups, plates, containers, and packaging. Send us your logo (AI/PDF/PNG format), and we'll share a digital mockup within 24 hours. Min order for custom printing: 5,000 pieces. One-time printing plate charges apply.
