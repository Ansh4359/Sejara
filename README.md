# Sejara Industries — Disposable Plates & Cups Manufacturer

Official website for **Sejara Industries**, a leading manufacturer and supplier of disposable plates, cups, and food packaging solutions.

## About Sejara Industries

Sejara Industries specializes in producing high-quality disposable tableware including:

- **Paper Plates** — Round, square, and compartment plates in various sizes
- **Paper Cups** — Hot cups, cold cups, and custom-printed cups
- **Food Packaging** — Clamshells, trays, and takeaway containers
- **Custom Solutions** — Branded and OEM products for businesses
